## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
> Focus Bear is an app/team created to help neurodivergent individuals, where there is a lack of apps specifically designed for them. 
## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
> Focus Bear is an app specifically designed for neurodivergent individuals, where other apps aren't. It is more hands on and direct, with very helpful features
## Why do you think this mission is important?
> I think its very important since there is a significant amount of neurodivergent individuals, and the numbers seem to be growing even more. This app can have the potential to improve the lives of many
## How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?
> My ideal career would be in a field/company that has a direct impact in improving/helping the community. I feel like being an intern here,  even for only a short period of time, will be a step in that direction. I want to integrate a sense of purpose where I work. 
## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
> I don't think I'm neurodivergent, but I've always had issues with being distracted all the time, procrastination, and lack of motivation in some cases,  hence I can relate to the challenges the app is trying to address.
