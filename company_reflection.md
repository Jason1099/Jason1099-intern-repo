# Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
> "Focus Bear is an anti-distraction and anti-burnout app for people with ADHD and ASD. The team behind Focus Bear is neurodivergent (ND) – we tried all the productivity and wellness apps out there and didn’t find them a good fit for our brains…so we built our own. Rates of ADHD diagnosis are skyrocketing at the moment due to increased awareness. The app is built in mind for ND folks (15 years and older) who need help focusing on their studies or work."

Focus Bear is an app/team created to help neurodivergent individuals, where there is a lack of apps specifically designed for them. 

Focus Bear also has sections for blogs, which I've found to be a nice addition to the website. I've read the "ADHD and Social Media Distraction: How to Stay Focused" blog, and definitely helped me. 

# What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Focus Bear is an app specifically designed for neurodivergent individuals, where other apps aren't. It is more hands on and direct, with very helpful features. Some features I find unique and helpful include:

1. Distraction blocking where we can specify what should be blocked or not.
2. Habit planner and routine tracker. I find this good to have, habits are difficult to get started on at the start, and this helps remind people to stick to them
3. A break reminder. This is a nice feature to have for a lot of people, where a majority are sitting on desks looking at screens. This gives that sense that the app is there for your benefit and we should remember to take care of ourselves.

# Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
I don't think I'm neurodivergent, but I've always had issues with being distracted all the time, procrastination, and lack of motivation in some cases, hence I can relate to the challenges the app is trying to address. 

"Doomscrolling" on social media platforms like Instagram and YouTube is a big problem for a lot of people if not controlled, and I've definitely experienced this. I didn't get work done in time in some cases because of that.
