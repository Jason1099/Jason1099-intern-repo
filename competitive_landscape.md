# --[Freedom](https://freedom.to/features)--
## Features
1. Block certain websites, apps, internet access.
2. Schedule Planning
3. A sound/music feature
4. Syncing across different devices

# --[Cold Turkey](https://getcoldturkey.com/features/)--
## Features
1. Block certain websites, apps, internet access. Gives access to block specific urls, youtube channels. 
2. Most features are locked behind a paywall, such as schedule blocks, breaks, application blocks.

# --[Tiimo](https://www.tiimoapp.com/product)--
Made with ADHD in mind
## Features
1. Visual Planner, with flexible planning assisted by AI
2. Customizable visuals
3. ADHD/ Neurodivergent specific features, such as the focus timer, widgets, timelines. 

# --[Routinery](https://www.routinery.app/)--
A routine planning app with timers, checklists and tags. A big feature would be the ability to share and download routines


# What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?
Focus bear shares alot of the features from the beforementioned apps. What makes it different is that it combines alot of the useful features from these apps. For example, Tiimo and Routinery are focused on planning habits and routines, where as Freedom and Cold Turkey are more focused on distraction blocking for planned sessions.<br/>

# If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?
This relates to my previous answer, where I would use the app because it combines the important features that exist in other apps, making it an "all in one" sort of app.  

# What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?
I think a big feature that the other apps (or some of them) have is the ability to plan schedule blocks in a table/calendar view. 

# Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?
My answer to this would relate to the previous answer, where the ability to plan a timetable and view specific routines or sessions related to completing a task for each day of the week would greatly improve the app.
As of now, I believe the app only provides a way to plan a daily routine and tasks (without a way to plan different sessions to complete it).<br/>
Integrating a free version for AI blocking and Planning could also be implemented, where it would give a taste for free users on how useful it is, which could push for more paying users.
