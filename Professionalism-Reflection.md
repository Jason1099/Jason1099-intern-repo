# Research
## What are examples of professional vs. unprofessional behaviour in a casual work environment
Communication wise:
1. Respectully wait for others to finish talking before talking yourself, vs cutting others
2. Clear, concise language, and be polite, vs being unfiltered and rude.

Tardiness:
1. Being punctual and meeting deadlines, vs always being late and disregarding deadlines

Conduct and Appearance:
1. Wear clean and neat clothing, without being too formal, vs using innapropriate clothing
2. Be respectful and polite in office situations, such as keeping noise levels down, and keeping cleanliness, vs being inconsiderate and disrupting everyone else's work.
